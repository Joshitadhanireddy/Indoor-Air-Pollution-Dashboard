Did You Know?

Air pollution is responsible for approximately 7 million premature deaths annually. Small changes in our daily habits can help reduce air pollution and protect our environment.

Interesting Facts

  • Indoor air pollution can be 2-5 times worse than outdoor air pollution.
  • Using public transport reduces your carbon footprint significantly.
  • Trees not only absorb CO2 but also filter harmful air pollutants.
  • Regular maintenance of vehicles can reduce harmful emissions.
